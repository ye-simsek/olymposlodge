Tower Penthouse
Apartment

The Tower Penthouse Apartment is a four-bedroom private residence equipped to the highest standards. The most luxurious hotel suite in the Swiss Alps, it occupies the top three floors of the majestic Palace tower. Enjoy magnificent views from the balconies and exquisite works of art.

Hans Badrutt Suite

Enter through an impressive marble lobby and discover a spacious living room with a grand piano, an elegantly appointed bedroom with a dressing room, and a marble bathroom with a Jacuzzi. Here, too, you will find one of the largest terraces at the Palace.

Helen Badrutt Suite

This large, elegant suite is a true St. Moritz icon, with expansive living quarters, a spacious bedroom, a bathroom clad in Italian marble, a separate drawing room, a kitchenette and a private balcony overlooking Lake St. Moritz.

Two-Bedroom St. Moritz Suite

The perfect option for families seeking the ultimate in comfort, this two-bedroom suite offers sumptuously inviting interiors and expansive lake and mountain views. Here, luxury meets serenity in your spacious home away from home.

Hitchcock Suite

St. Moritz Suite

An expansive balcony bathes the suite in natural daylight and serves up some of the best views in St. Moritz, making it a great spot for both relaxing and entertaining.

Palace Suite

Serlas Five-Bedroom
Penthouse Suite

The Serlas Five-Bedroom Penthouse Suite offers a residential experience of the highest caliber. Here, your luxurious home away from home blends seamlessly with the vast mountain landscape. This exclusive penthouse occupies the top section of the newly designed wing by ACPV Architects, ensuring the utmost privacy while incorporating exquisite Loro Piana Interiors materials and the full range of grand hotel amenities.

Book View more

Serlas Three-Bedroom
Penthouse Suite

The Serlas Three-Bedroom Penthouse Apartment offers the highest level of comfort and elegance. Here, guests can enjoy the spaciousness of a grand suite along with the full service and amenities of a luxury hotel. Showcasing the finest elements of Loro Piana Interiors, the Serlas Three-Bedroom Penthouse Suite presents a spectacular view over St. Moritz and its surrounding mountains. The elevator provides direct access to the shopping opportunities at the Palace Galerie.

Serlas Two-Bedroom
Penthouse Suite

Experience the supreme comfort and spaciousness of a suite with the ultimate service and amenities of a grand hotel. The Serlas Two-Bedroom Penthouse Suite features the finest Loro Piana Interiors materials and offers a breathtaking, one-of-a-kind view from the top floor of the ACPV Architects-designed building. Guests enjoy direct and effortless access via an elevator to the luxury boutiques of the Palace Galerie.

Serlas Two-Bedroom
Suite

Offering unparalleled luxury, the 159-square-meter Serlas Two-Bedroom Suite is an open, airy, light-filled retreat with plenty of room for those who like to spread out. It presents the finest materials from Loro Piana and crisp, inspiring modern design that makes for a truly elegant Alpine retreat. The suite’s large living room is beautifully complemented by two bedrooms, each with its own private bathroom.

Serlas Prestige Suite

A pinnacle of luxury, taste and sophisticated design, the spacious Serlas Prestige Suite presents Loro Piana Interiors at their best. The suite’s exclusivity, ambiance and access to the world’s best shopping at Palace Galerie make it the ideal home-away-from-home for the most discerning guest.

Serlas Suite

Experience unmatched comfort in an elegantly designed setting that boasts top, handcrafted Loro Piana fabrics. With easy access to The Palace Galerie on Via Serlas, where all the luxury stores are located, The Serlas Suite is a sublimely private and a peaceful retreat that presents a warm blend of oak-wood accents, stone flooring and wool-fabric wall paneling from Loro Piana.

Signature Double Room
Lake View with Balcony
