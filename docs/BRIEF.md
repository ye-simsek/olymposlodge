# Olympos Lodge — Creative & Editorial Brief

## Property

**Olympos Lodge**, Çıralı, Ulupınar Mahallesi, Kemer / Antalya, Turkey.
Established 1987. A boutique ecolodge on one of the Mediterranean's most protected beaches — no cars, no development, a national park at the back. Minutes from the ancient Lycian city of Olympos and the Chimaera eternal flames.

6 room categories across a 20,000 m² garden. Exclusively for guests: beach, restaurant, garden, spa.

## Target guest

Design-conscious slow traveller. Values authenticity, nature, and craft over amenities checklists. Drawn to places with a story — not manufactured luxury but earned character. Likely discovered the property through editorial travel media or word of mouth.

## Tone of voice

Quiet. Unhurried. Poetic but grounded — no purple prose, no exclamation marks. Let the place speak. The writing should feel like the hotel: something has been stripped away, and what remains is finer for it.

Avoid: "world-class", "luxurious", "unforgettable", "nestled".
Prefer: specific detail, sensory language, restraint.

## Design references

**Casa Angelina** (Praiano, Italy) — graphic restraint, white volumes, editorial photography.
**Amanruya** (Bodrum, Turkey) — materiality, space, quiet confidence.
