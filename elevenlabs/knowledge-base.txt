# Olympos Lodge — Knowledge Base

## Hotel Overview

Olympos Lodge is a boutique hotel established in 1987, located in Çıralı, on the Lycian coast of Turkey. Open all year round. 17 rooms spread across a 20,000 m² themed garden. Single-storey architecture. Direct beach access — the Mediterranean is 30 metres away.

Website: www.olymposlodge.com.tr
Address: Ulupınar Mah. Çıralı Sok. No:9 Kemer / Antalya
Phone: +90 242 825 71 71
Email: info@olymposlodge.com.tr

Çıralı is a protected coastal area — no high-rises, no beach clubs, no overdevelopment. The hotel is directly on the Caretta Caretta–protected beach and within walking distance of the ancient city of Olympos and the Chimera (Yanartaş) eternal flames.

The hotel welcomes families with children, couples, solo travellers, and adults of all ages. Pets (cats and dogs) are accepted free of charge; leashes are requested in common areas.

## Reservation and Payment

100% upfront payment is required at the time of reservation. Payment on arrival is not available. Accepted methods: credit card or bank transfer.

Reservations are non-refundable. Date changes may be made subject to availability. If a date change is not possible, the reservation can be kept on hold for one year — the existing balance can be used at current prices. In cases of loss of a first-degree relative or serious health issues, a date change can be made for use within one year; the payment will be recalculated based on the pricing of the new dates.

For bank transfer reservations, the total fee must be received within 24 hours or the reservation is automatically cancelled.

All prices include room, breakfast, tax, and service charge. Prices on the official website and through direct inquiries are the best available.

## Nearby Attractions

Olympos Ancient City: 800 m, approximately 10 minutes on foot. Lycian, Roman, and Byzantine ruins including tombs, a theatre, and city walls.

Chimera (Yanartaş): 3.5 km, approximately 10 minutes by car or 45 minutes on foot. Eternal flames burning for over 2,500 years — the inspiration for Homer's Chimaera myth.

Phaselis Ancient City: 25 km, approximately 30 minutes by car. Uniquely positioned city with three harbours.

Tahtalı Cable Car (Olympos Teleferik): approximately 40 minutes by car. Ascent to 2,365 metres with panoramic views over the Mediterranean, Çıralı, and Kemer. Open April–November.

Lycian Way: one of the world's top long-distance hiking trails (540 km total). The Olympos–Adrasan section passes near the hotel. Ideal seasons: April–June and September–November.

## Getting Here

Most guests fly into Antalya Airport (AYT). The hotel is approximately 90 km south along the D400 highway, then a 7 km descent through an emerald-green valley into Çıralı.

Airport transfer: private door-to-door service, €100 one-way per vehicle. Share your arrival time with the hotel to arrange.

By car: follow D400 from Kemer or Kumluca direction, follow Çıralı signs. The entire road to the hotel entrance is paved.

Parking: free, private, monitored by security cameras. EV charging station available. Valet parking upon request.

## Accessibility

All rooms are on the ground floor. No stairs anywhere on the property. Common areas are spacious with wide, level paths. The hotel entrance and all room entrances are illuminated and designed without threshold differences. No dedicated disabled-access rooms, but wheelchair users and guests with limited mobility can move comfortably throughout the hotel.


## Health and Safety

24-hour security personnel on duty. All common areas monitored by security cameras. All rooms equipped with smoke detectors and fire extinguishers. Emergency exit directions and assembly areas are designated.

For minor health needs, the hotel vehicle transports guests to the nearest healthcare facility. In emergencies, a doctor can be directed to the premises and ambulance support is provided.


## Mosquitoes

Mosquito repellent sprays and plug-in repellent devices are provided. All rooms are equipped with mosquito nets.


## What Is Not Here

No televisions in rooms. The garden has been filling that role since 1987.
No swimming pool. The Mediterranean is 30 metres away.
No all-inclusive package. The restaurant serves à la carte with fresh, seasonal ingredients.
No kids' club or children's animation programme. The garden, beach, and nature are the playground.
No gym or fitness centre. Activities are outdoors — cycling, canoeing, SUP, hiking.
No nightlife or loud entertainment. Çıralı is a protected quiet zone; evenings centre around the fire pit, the library, and stargazing.
No elevator or lift. The hotel is single-storey — one is not needed.
No dedicated disabled-access rooms. All rooms are ground floor with no stairs, and common areas have wide, level paths.


## Conservation

Çıralı is a protected coastal area. Noise and light regulations are observed in accordance with conservation and quiet zone rules. The beach is in the Caretta Caretta (loggerhead turtle) protection zone.
